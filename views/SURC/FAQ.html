What is Shaastra Ultimate Robotics Championship?

Shaastra Ultimate Robotics Championship is an international robotics competition that is organised by IIT Madras as a part of its annual technical fest, Shaastra 2017.

How do we participate in Shaastra Ultimate Robotics Championship?

To register, participants must fill the registration form which is available on the official Shaastra Ultimate Robotics Championship website. Each team can have a maximum of 6 members.

Do international teams have to pay Rs 600 for registration fee before CDR submissions?

No, Only after announcement of CDR results teams have to pay Rs 600 per team for participating in Finals.

Can we have inter-college teams?

No, you cannot have inter-college teams. Participants have to be from the same college/institute.

Can multiple teams participate from the same Institute/College?

Yes, multiple teams can participate from the same Institute/College.

How do you decide the nationality of the team?

The nationality of the team will be decided on basis of the nationality of the institute the team comes from.

Will accomodation be provided during SHAASTRA?

Yes, accomodation will be provided to all participants during SHAASTRA.

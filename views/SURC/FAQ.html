What is Shaastra Ultimate Robotics Challenge?

Shaastra Ultimate Robotics Challenge is an all India robotics competition that is organised by IIT Madras as a part of its annual technical fest, Shaastra 2017.

What is the timeline?

Shaastra Ultimate Robotics Challenge will be held in two stages, the prelims and the mains. The prelims will be held in October in all the major cities across India. The mains will be conducted in IIT Madras during Shaastra 2017.

How do we participate in Shaastra Ultimate Robotics Challenge?

To register, participants must fill the registration form which is available on the official Shaastra Ultimate Robotics Challenge website. Each team can have a maximum of 5 members. Registration fees for each team has to be paid on the day of the event at the respective centres.

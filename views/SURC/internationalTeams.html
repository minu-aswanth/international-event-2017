Event Format for International Teams

International teams consist of the teams participating in the event which are not from India.

  • CDR: Critical Design Review

    In this round the participating teams have to make a video and a document submission for CDR problem statement given in the problem statement tab of this website. From the CDR submissions, teams would be shortlisted and invited to the Mains of SURC at Shaastra 2017.

  • Finals

    Finals of SURC will be held at IIT Madras during its Annual Technical Festival SHAASTRA where participants will go all guns blazing at finest robotics enthusiasts across the world. The problem statement of the Finals will be declared soon. Participants will also get a chance to witness and participate in various other events which will be conducted during these 5 days.

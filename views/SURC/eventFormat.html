Indian Teams

  • Zonals

    The Zonals will be conducted in selected major cities across the nation except in IIT Madras where it would be conducted during Shaastra. Students can participate in a team of maximum 6 members. The top performers in the Zonals will be invited to IIT Madras to participate in the Finals of the competition to be held during Shaastra ‘17.

  • Finals

    Finals of SURC will be held at IIT Madras during its Annual Technical Festival SHAASTRA where participants will go all guns blazing at finest robotics enthusiasts from the country as well as across the world. The Problem Statement of the Finals will be an advanced version of Zonals and would be released on the website soon. Participants will also get a chance to witness and participate in various other events which will be conducted during these 5 days.

International Teams

International teams consist of the teams participating in the event which are not from India.

  • CDR: Critical Design Review

    In this round the participating teams have to make a video and a document submission for CDR given in the Problem Statement tab of this website. From the CDR submissions, teams would be shortlisted and invited to the Finals of SURC at Shaastra 2017.

  • Finals

    Finals of SURC will be held at IIT Madras during its Annual Technical Festival SHAASTRA where participants will go all guns blazing at finest robotics enthusiasts across the world. The Problem Statement of the Finals will be declared soon. Participants will also get a chance to witness and participate in various other events which will be conducted during these 5 days.
